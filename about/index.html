Hi! My name is Davis Land.

I'm a student at Texas A&M and was formerly an intern at Atlantic Public Media in Woods Hole, MA. I like making things.

I have an interest in all things media, whether that is sound, video, print, or whatever crazy holodeck stuff I'm sure will be invented in a few years.

I currently serve as the Editor-In-Chief of two publications: The Eckleburg Project, which is A&M's student-run literary journal, and Alien Mouth, an independent journal and press I started with my friend.

I've also served as the Programming Director for KANM Student Radio for a good while now, as well as djing and volunteering at KEOS Community Radio.

In the past I've run a podcast for Write About Now Poetry, as well as produced my own content for various freelance reporting gigs.
