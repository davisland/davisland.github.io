Hi! My name is Davis Land.

I'm a student at Texas A&M University, studying Telecommunications and Media Studies.

I like to make stuff.

I was formerly an intern at Atlantic Public Media in Woods Hole, MA. I've also worked on a bunch of other projects with different levels and sizes of organizations. Currently I serve as the Editor-In-Chief for two publications: The Eckleburg Project, which is A&M's student-run literary journal, and Alien Mouth, an independent journal and press I started with my friend.

I work for the Texas A&M University Writing Center where I produce a podcast called Write Right. I've also served as the Programming Director for KANM Student Radio for a good while now, while djing and volunteering at KEOS Community Radio. I also produce a podcast for Write About Now Poetry, which is in its third season of production.
